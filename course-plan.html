Course Plan

This page provides detailed course plans for undergraduate and postgraduate programs at IIT Madras.

Undergraduate Programs

  • Computer Science and Engineering: 8 semesters covering core CS topics, electives, and projects.
  • Electrical Engineering: 8 semesters with focus on circuits, systems, and applications.
  • Mechanical Engineering: 8 semesters including design, manufacturing, and thermodynamics.
  • Civil Engineering: 8 semesters covering structures, geotechnical, and environmental engineering.
  • Chemical Engineering: 8 semesters with emphasis on process engineering and materials.
  • Aerospace Engineering: 8 semesters focusing on aerodynamics, propulsion, and control.

Postgraduate Programs

  • M.Tech in various disciplines with coursework and thesis components.
  • Ph.D. programs with research focus in specialized areas.
