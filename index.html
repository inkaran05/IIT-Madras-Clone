About IIT Madras

IIT Madras is one of India's premier institutions, globally recognized for academic excellence and cutting-edge research. Established in 1959, it is home to top faculty, brilliant students, and world-class infrastructure.

Academics

IIT Madras offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, sciences, management, and humanities. The curriculum is designed to foster innovation and research.

Campus Life

Experience a vibrant campus filled with student clubs, festivals, sports, and serene nature. IIT Madras has a unique campus located within a national park, offering a peaceful and inspiring environment.

Admissions

Admission to IIT Madras is through national-level exams like JEE Advanced and GATE. Explore undergraduate, postgraduate, and research opportunities on our official portal.

Events

From technical fests like Shaastra to cultural festivals like Saarang, IIT Madras hosts a range of exciting events that blend learning and fun.
