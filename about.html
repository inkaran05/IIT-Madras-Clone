History and Vision

Established in 1959, IIT Madras is one of the premier institutes of engineering and technology in India. It aims to provide world-class education, foster research and innovation, and contribute to the nation's development.

Mission

  • To advance knowledge in science, engineering, and technology.
  • To nurture creativity and innovation among students and faculty.
  • To collaborate with industry and academia globally.
  • To contribute to sustainable development and societal welfare.

About All IITs
